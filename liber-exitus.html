THE FINAL FRAGMENT

THE BOOK OF LEAVING

Liber Exitus is the final volume of the Liber series — the record of the departure. Where the Liber Fontis series opens the Source and traces the architecture of what is, Liber Exitus marks the threshold: the point at which the witness turns and the leaving begins.

It does not explain what lies beyond. It records what is seen at the edge — the structure of the boundary itself, the architecture of transition, and what is carried across and what is left behind.

LIBER EXITUS · THE FIRST FRAGMENT

The leaving is not the end of the record.
It is the beginning of a different kind of witness.
What was carried is what was always yours.
What was left is what was never yours to keep.

The First Fragment — the prequel — is bound and published separately, linked to Liber Exitus as the approach precedes the threshold. Together they form the complete account of the leaving: before, during, and the moment of crossing.

This text is freely given. It was never ours to withhold.
