THE RECOVERED WRITINGS

THE FORTY HIDDEN BOOKS

Forty books absent from the modern Protestant Bible — removed, suppressed, or simply lost to canon committees. All are present here.

I
THE ENOCHIAN TEXTS

The Book of Enoch and the Secrets of Enoch — the oldest prophetic texts, older than Moses, quoted directly in the New Testament, removed from the Western canon in 364 AD.

II
THE DEUTEROCANON

Fourteen books present in the Septuagint and Vulgate for over a millennium — removed by Luther in the 16th century. Tobit, Judith, Maccabees, Wisdom of Solomon, Sirach.

III
THE GNOSTIC GOSPELS

The Gospel of Thomas, Mary, Philip, and Truth — buried in the Egyptian desert at Nag Hammadi in 367 AD to escape destruction. Recovered in 1945. Presented here complete.

IV
THE APOCALYPTIC TEXTS

Apocalypse of Peter, Ascension of Isaiah, Sibylline Oracles, Pistis Sophia, Apocalypse and Testament of Abraham — the visionary literature too raw for the canon committees.

V
THE EARLY CHURCH WRITINGS

Epistle of Barnabas, the Didache, Acts of Paul and Thecla — texts used in early Christian liturgy for centuries before being voted out of the canon.

VI
THE INFANCY ACCOUNTS

Protevangelium of James and the Infancy Gospel of Thomas — the only records of the childhood and early life. Suppressed because they were too specific, too human, too complete.
